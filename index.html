RUNNER'S SAID

At 2017 -- I went to Italy, Venice for holiday and of course I also participated the international marathon event at this beautiful city. That day was so cold in the morning, 7am at temperature of 6 degrees celsius and I'm with my thick jacket, first time I run like that very cute and very clumsy. I'm not just fighting for the unfamiliar weather but also the unfamiliar terrain, I'm the only one Malaysian in this race, but the way I received the support from the people that I can call total strangers, really make me feel warm from the inside when they refuelling me by their cheer. I really felt touching and they made me felt they traded me like their friend.. “ don’t easily give up!! ” this is the way they cheer for me, and I still can hear it sometime from my ears, I can't forget that even now, I believed that's is one of the reason I finished the 10 km marathon in this cold but yet warm beautiful city, Venice. I will continue to train and run, I hope I will be ready when I see the new challenge again..

Thom
