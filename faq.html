FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

home | Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a virtual race?

A virtual race is a “race” that can be done in anywhere without specific starting time and place. The program may set specific dates or place occasionally, sometimes the period will be set in between one or two week to finish. Just pick your favorite running path or trail and start RUNNING.

2. What is the distance for virtual races?

They can be ANY distance!! Base on which race event you join.

3. What if I can’t complete the entire distance yet?

We never encourage you to push too hard to yourself, because it will be forfeitedthe purpose to get healthy by simple running. Remember the virtual race, pacedoesn’t matter. If you joined the 5km run but only done it ⅗ and the rest of 2km youare just walking.. basically you are still hero to us and of course you are eligible to claim the medal by simply submitting your result to us.

We do not wish to get inform by any participate during the race there are any injury or accident happen, so please know your limits and be safe during the race event especially for the longer distance run like 10km, half marathon or full marathon race.

So set your goal and build it slowly, the most important is continue the journey of running.
